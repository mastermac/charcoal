VOLANT.

Our singular purpose was to create a product

not bound by convention. Volant is the realization

of that dream. Inspired by 21st century trends

in aeronautics, we brought Volant to life and

integrated it with cutting edge technology.

Imagined with simple yet purposeful strokes,

the body is constructed out of virtually seamless

lines of Hi-Macs® - marrying the look of classic

stone with the avant grade form.

A story of extraordinary engineering,

For Volant to exist as it does, our engineers and designers had to create a light-weight frame strong enough to withstand an incredible 2,500 N-m of torque and yet ensure that Volant remains aesthetically stunning.

Drawing lessons from the cutting edge in aeronautics and automobile engineering, that's exactly what they did.

Change the height with just a word or at the touch of a button.

The Intelligence.

Volant has been outfitted with a powerful processor, seamlessly combining Charcoal's proprietary software and hardware. Software updates will introduce new features and improve existing functionality, making the product more capable over time.

In the future, Volant will learn how it's used and accordingly remind the user to change positions. The updates will also enable intelligent health-based analytics by syncing with phones and fitbits.
