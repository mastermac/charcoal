VOLANT.

Our singular purpose was to create a product not bound by convention. Volant is the realization of that dream. Inspired by 21st century trends in aeronautics, we brought Volant to life using distinctive materials and integrated it with cutting edge technology.

Imagined with simple yet purposeful strokes, the body is constructed out of virtually seamless lines of Hi-Macs® - marrying the look of classic stone with avant grade form.

A story of extraordinary engineering,

For Volant to exist as it does, our engineers and designers had to create a light-weight frame strong enough to withstand an incredible 2,500 N-m of torque and yet ensure that Volant remains aesthetically stunning.

Drawing lessons from the cutting edge in aeronautics and automobile engineering, that's exactly what they did.
