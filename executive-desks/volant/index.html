VOLANT.

Our singular purpose was to create a product

not bound by convention. Inspired by 21st century

trends in aeronautics, Volant is the realization of

this dream.

Imagined with simple yet purposeful strokes,

the body is constructed out of virtually seamless

lines of HI-MACS® - marrying the look of classic

stone with the avant-grade form.

A story of extraordinary engineering,

For Volant to exist as it does, our engineers and designers had to create a light-weight frame strong enough to withstand an incredible 2,500 N-m of torque, and yet ensure that Volant remains aesthetically stunning.

Drawing lessons from the cutting edge in aeronautics and automobile engineering, that's exactly what they did.

Change the height with just a word or at the touch of a button.
